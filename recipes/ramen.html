Pork Ramen

Photo taken from unsplash.com

Ingredients

    Main dish:

  • 4 slices of pork, simmered
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cm leek (white part), sliced diagonally
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • pepper
  • 100 g ramen noodles
  • spring onion (scallion), chopped

    • Broth

  • 400 ml water
  • 4 tablespoon sauce from the pork
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cut pork into thin pieces.
  2. Boil the egg. It can be marinated (optional) with the leftover sauce from the pork. Marinade it up to 2 hours.
  3. Put soy sauce, few pinches of pepper, leek and sesame oil in a bowl and mix it.
  4. Put all the broth ingredients in a small pot and set in on medium heat until it boils. Afterwards turn the heat down to the minimum to keep the broth warm.
  5. Cook the noodles.
  6. Pour the broth over the noodles, add the toppings and scallions with some pepper.

Tip: You want to serve the dish as soon as possible so the noodles won't get soggy. Enjoy!
